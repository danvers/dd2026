## Travel

### By Plane

Frankfurt Airport (FRA) is one of Europe's largest international airports and offers direct connections to destinations worldwide.

From the airport, Campus Westend can be reached in approximately **25–35 minutes** by public transport or taxi.

### By Train

Frankfurt (Main) Hauptbahnhof (Central Station) is a major hub for German and international high-speed rail services (ICE).

From the central station, Campus Westend can be reached in approximately **15–20 minutes** using public transport.

### Public Transport

Frankfurt has an extensive public transport network operated by RMV. Campus Westend is easily accessible by subway (Underground), tram and bus.

The closest stations (Underground) include:

- **Holzhausenstraße** (U1, U2, U3, U8)
- **Miquel-/Adickesallee** (U1, U2, U3, U8)

From both stations, the campus is within a short walking distance. We recommend using public transport whenever possible.
